PUBG studio has launched its new version for mobile devices called PUBG: New State. This game is solely developed by Krafton Inclusive and hence does not have any link with Tencent. It was in hype a month ago by the name PUBG 2 but now PUBG has officially dropped its trailer and pre-registration link too. Pre-registered user will get free permanent vehicle skin of this new game. Click here to Pre-register the game PUBG: NEW STATE
Note:Pre-registration for IOS users haven't started as of now and might open soon.
Looking at the trailer game seems to be amazing.This game brings the new map named Troi with whole new feel of battle royale. The game has very realistic graphics and have new weapons, drones, and vehicles too.
